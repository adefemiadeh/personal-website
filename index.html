Adefemi O. Adeyanju

I am a PhD researcher in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence at the University of Ibadan, Nigeria, with research interests centered on medical imaging, intelligent decision-making systems, and agentic AI. My work lies at the intersection of advanced computational methods and real-world problem-solving, particularly in healthcare and data-driven systems.


I have advanced training in machine learning and applied artificial intelligence, with research experience spanning deep learning, probabilistic modeling, optimization methods, and intelligent agents. My doctoral research focuses on developing robust and efficient learning models for medical image analysis, including disease detection and classification, with particular emphasis on model performance optimization, reliability, and interpretability.


My scholarly interests extend beyond model accuracy to broader questions of trust, transparency, and deployment in high-stakes environments. I am especially motivated by understanding how AI systems can be designed to reason, adapt, and support decision-making in complex and uncertain settings.
