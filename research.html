Research Interests

My research focuses on Machine Learning, Large Language Models, Medical Imaging, and Agentic AI. I design adaptive and intelligent systems for real-world applications in healthcare and education.

  • Machine Learning
  • Medical Image Analysis
  • Large Language Models
  • Agentic AI Systems

Medical Imaging

Medical imaging is a central application domain of my research, where I investigate deep learning models for disease detection, classification, and clinical decision support. My work emphasizes data-efficient learning and rigorous model evaluation, addressing challenges such as limited data availability, class imbalance, and domain variability.


A core component of this research involves hyperparameter optimization and model selection, particularly through Bayesian optimization and related probabilistic techniques. These methods enable systematic performance improvements while managing computational cost, which is critical in large-scale medical imaging experiments.


In parallel, I explore explainable AI techniques to improve model transparency and trust. By integrating interpretability and uncertainty-aware modeling, my research aims to bridge the gap between high-performing AI systems and their practical adoption in clinical workflows

Agentic AI Systems

My second major research direction focuses on agentic AI, where artificial intelligence systems are designed to act autonomously, reason over goals, and adapt to dynamic environments. This work moves beyond static prediction models toward AI systems capable of long-term planning, interaction, and continuous learning.


I investigate how machine learning models, large language models, and optimization techniques can be integrated to build intelligent agents that support decision-making under uncertainty. Particular emphasis is placed on agentic systems in structured and high-stakes domains such as healthcare, scientific research, and complex system management.


Through this research, I aim to contribute frameworks for intelligent agents that combine data-driven learning with goal-directed reasoning, supporting scalable, transparent, and human-aligned AI systems.
